Instructions

You will now begin the face perception task.

You will see 35 individual faces.

For each face:
  • Toggle Side
    Use the Toggle Side button to flip between the left and right side of the face. Toggle as many times as you like.
  • Rate Trustworthiness
    Use the 1–9 scale to rate how trustworthy each side appears (1 = Not at all trustworthy, 9 = Extremely trustworthy).
  • Rate Emotion
    Use the 1–9 scale to rate the emotion each side expresses (1 = Very Negative, 9 = Very Positive).
  • Compare Masculinity and Femininity
    You'll be asked:
    • Which side appears more masculine?
    • Which side appears more feminine?
  • Final Rating
    You'll then see the full face and rate its overall trustworthiness, emotion, and masculinity/femininity.

Important: Take your time and toggle back and forth to get a good impression before rating.
