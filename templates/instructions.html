Instructions

Welcome to the Face Perception Task.
You will view 35 faces, each presented across two screens.

🔁 Screen 1: Hemiface Ratings (Left & Right)

  • Toggle Side
    Use the "Toggle Side" button to flip between the left and right sides of the face. You may toggle back and forth as many times as needed.
  • Rate Trustworthiness
    Use the 1-9 scale to rate how trustworthy the current side appears.
    (1 = Not at all trustworthy, 9 = Extremely trustworthy)
  • Rate Emotion
    Use the 1-9 scale to rate the emotion expressed by the current side.
    (1 = Very negative, 9 = Very positive)
  • Compare Masculinity and Femininity
    After viewing both sides, answer:
    • Which side appears more masculine?
    • Which side appears more feminine?

🦱 Screen 2: Full-Face View

  • Rate overall trustworthiness (1-9).
  • Rate overall emotional expression (1-9).
  • Which side appears more masculine?
  • Which side appears more feminine?

⚠️ Reminder

Take your time toggling between sides on Screen 1 to form a clear impression before answering.
You can return to this instruction screen at any time during the task using the "Instructions" button (Top Right Corner on the next screen).
